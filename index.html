The Context The Situation

America is fighting overseas in the Great War but there is also conflict at home. Women are yearning for equal treatment and suffrage — the right to vote. Numerous women who have spoken out have been silenced & imprisoned. Help women gain voting rights by winning over public opinion and put pressure on government leaders to pass a new amendment to secure suffrage.

Your Mission

You have been handed 5 stages of puzzles that you must solve. As you solve each puzzle you will be provided numbers that will eventually reveal a phone number to dial. This will be the last push needed to lift the final restraints and pass the 19th Amendment — granting suffrage. Hurry, there is no time to waste. American women need your help.

"It's puzzling that someone's gender can be the reason why they are treated unequally."
Collect clues from each puzzle to unlock the final restraints and grant suffrage for all women
